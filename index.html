Healthy Foods During Your Period

1. Iron-Rich Foods

  • Spinach, methi (fenugreek), amaranth leaves
  • Broccoli
  • Beetroot, carrots
  • Dates, raisins, prunes
  • Pomegranates, apples, bananas
  • Lentils (masoor, moong, arhar dal), chickpeas, rajma
  • Jaggery (gur)
  • Sesame seeds (til)

2. Magnesium-Rich Foods

  • Bananas
  • Almonds, cashews, walnuts
  • Dark chocolate (70% cocoa or more)
  • Pumpkin, flax, and sunflower seeds
  • Avocado

3. Calcium-Rich Foods

  • Milk, yogurt, paneer
  • Cheese (in moderation)
  • Soy products like tofu
  • Almond milk or fortified plant-based milk
  • Sesame seeds

Foods to Avoid

  • Excess salt (causes bloating)
  • Caffeine (worsens cramps)
  • Fried and junk foods (increases inflammation)
  • Sugary snacks (causes energy crashes)
Take care of yourself, Mahu! Stay healthy and comfortable.
